Our University Ranking Methodology

At WhosBest.org, we employ a comprehensive, data-driven approach to evaluate universities across multiple dimensions of excellence. Our methodology combines quantitative metrics with qualitative assessments to provide a holistic view of each institution's strengths and achievements.

Data Collection

We gather data from multiple sources, including institutional submissions, government databases, academic surveys, bibliometric databases, and student satisfaction surveys. All data undergoes rigorous verification to ensure accuracy and currency.

Evaluation Criteria

Universities are evaluated across five key dimensions: Academic Excellence (30%), Research Impact (25%), Student Experience (20%), Global Impact (15%), and Career Outcomes (10%). Each dimension comprises multiple specific metrics designed to capture different aspects of university performance.

Scoring System

Each metric is standardized to enable fair comparisons across institutions of different sizes and types. Weighted scores are calculated for each dimension and combined to produce an overall score on a scale of 0-10, determining the final ranking position.

Expert Review

A panel of education experts reviews the preliminary rankings to provide qualitative insights and identify any anomalies that require further investigation. This human oversight ensures that our data-driven approach captures nuances that numbers alone might miss.

Annual Updates

Rankings are updated annually to reflect the latest data and developments in higher education. Our methodology is continuously refined to ensure it remains relevant and accurately captures what constitutes excellence in the evolving landscape of higher education.
