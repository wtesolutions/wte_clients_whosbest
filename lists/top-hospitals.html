Our Hospital Ranking Methodology

WhosBest.org's hospital rankings are based on a comprehensive analysis of multiple factors that determine the quality of care and overall excellence of medical institutions. Our methodology includes:

Patient Outcomes (35%)

Measures of survival rates, complication rates, readmission rates, and other clinical outcomes across multiple conditions and procedures.

Quality of Care (25%)

Assessment of safety measures, infection control, nurse staffing levels, and adherence to clinical guidelines and best practices.

Patient Experience (15%)

Patient satisfaction scores, communication effectiveness, responsiveness of staff, and overall hospital environment.

Innovation & Research (15%)

Research output, clinical trials, adoption of advanced technologies, and contributions to medical knowledge.

Specialty Excellence (10%)

Performance in specialized areas of medicine, including complex procedures and treatments for rare conditions.

Expert Opinion (5%)

Surveys of medical professionals regarding hospital reputation and quality of care.

Data is collected from multiple sources, including Medicare data, patient surveys, medical databases, and direct hospital reporting. Our rankings are updated annually to reflect the most current information available.
